Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

Is your cleaning staff trained and experienced?

Yes, our cleaning staff is highly trained, experienced, and dedicated to providing top-quality cleaning services. We conduct thorough background checks and ensure our team is well-equipped to deliver outstanding results.

Are your cleaning products safe for my family and pets?

Absolutely! We use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Our products are effective at cleaning while being gentle on surfaces and safe for everyone in your home.

How often should I schedule cleaning services?

The frequency depends on your specific needs and lifestyle. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or one-time cleaning services. We'll work with you to determine the best schedule for your home or office.

Can I customize the cleaning services to fit my requirements?

Yes! We offer customizable cleaning packages to meet your specific needs. You can choose which areas to focus on, specific tasks to include or exclude, and any special requirements you may have.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel a cleaning appointment?

We understand that schedules can change. You can reschedule or cancel your appointment with at least 24 hours notice. Contact us as soon as possible to make any changes to your appointment.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, we are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. This protects both you and our team in the unlikely event of any accidents or damages during the cleaning process.
